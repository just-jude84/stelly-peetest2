Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a school tour?

You can schedule a school tour by filling out the contact form above, selecting "School Tour Request" as the subject. Alternatively, you can call our admissions office at (555) 123-4567 during office hours.

What are the school hours?

Regular school hours are from 8:30 AM to 3:30 PM, Monday through Friday. Early drop-off is available starting at 7:30 AM, and after-school programs run until 6:00 PM.

How can I apply for admission?

Our admissions process begins with an online application, which can be found on our Admissions page. After submitting the application, our admissions team will contact you to schedule an interview and campus tour.

Do you offer financial aid or scholarships?

Yes, Stelly-Pee School offers need-based financial aid and merit scholarships. Please contact our financial aid office at financialaid@stellypee.edu for more information about eligibility and application procedures.
