About Our School

Stelly-Pee School was established in 2016 with a vision to provide quality education that fosters academic excellence, character development, and lifelong learning.

Our dedicated faculty and state-of-the-art facilities create an environment where students can thrive both academically and personally.

We believe in holistic education that balances rigorous academics with arts, sports, and character development.

Our alumni have gone on to excel in various fields, making us proud of the foundation we provided them.

Our Programs

Elementary Program

Our elementary program focuses on building strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, and social skills through engaging, hands-on learning experiences.

Middle School Program

Our middle school curriculum challenges students academically while supporting their social and emotional development during these critical years.

High School Program

Our college-preparatory high school program offers rigorous academics, advanced placement courses, and extensive extracurricular opportunities.
