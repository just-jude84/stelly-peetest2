About Stelly-Pee School

Established in 2016, StellyPee School has been at the forefront of providing quality education that balances academic rigor with character development.

Our campus is on two sites, the primary and the college sites, all within the same vicinity with facilities that provide an ideal environment for learning and growth. We believe in educating the whole child - intellectually, socially, emotionally, and physically.

With a student-teacher ratio of 12:1, we ensure personalized attention for every student, helping them unlock their full potential.
