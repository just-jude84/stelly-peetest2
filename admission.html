Admissions at Stelly-Pee School

Begin your journey to excellence. Discover how to join our vibrant learning community.

Thank you for your interest in Stelly-Pee School! We are delighted that you are considering our school for your child's education. Our admissions process is designed to help us get to know your family and ensure that StellyPee is the right fit for your educational goals.

Admissions Requirements

Application Form

Complete online application with student and parent information.

Academic Records

Transcripts or report cards from the previous two years.

Assessment

Age-appropriate academic assessment for grade level placement.

Family Interview

Meeting with admissions staff to discuss educational goals and expectations.

Character References

Two references from previous teachers or mentors (for grades 5-12).

Financial Aid & Scholarships

Making Quality Education Accessible

At StellyPee School, we believe that financial circumstances should not be a barrier to quality education. We offer need-based financial aid and merit scholarships to qualified students.

Our financial aid program is designed to assist families who demonstrate financial need. Awards are granted based on a family's specific financial situation and the availability of funds.

Merit scholarships are available for students who demonstrate exceptional academic achievement, artistic talent, or athletic ability.

Learn More About Financial Aid

Merit Scholarships

Awarded to students who demonstrate exceptional academic performance or talents in arts, athletics, or leadership.

Need-Based Aid

Financial assistance for families who demonstrate economic need through our financial assessment process.

Payment Plans

Flexible payment options that allow tuition to be paid in monthly installments rather than one lump sum.

Sibling Discounts

Tuition discounts for families with multiple children enrolled at StellyPee School.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the application deadline?

We have rolling admissions, but priority is given to applications received by January 31st for the following academic year. Applications received after this date will be considered based on space availability.

When will we receive an admissions decision?

Admissions decisions are typically communicated within two weeks of completing the application process, including the student assessment and family interview.

Do you accept students during the school year?

Yes, we accept mid-year transfers if space is available in the desired grade level. The admissions process is the same for mid-year applicants.

Is there an application fee?

Yes, there is a non-refundable application fee of $75. This fee is waived for families applying for financial aid.

What are the age requirements for Kindergarten?

Children must be 5 years old by September 1st of the enrollment year to be eligible for our Kindergarten program.
